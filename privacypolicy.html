Website Visitors

Like most website operators, ABC collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. From time to time, ABC may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

Certain visitors to ABC’s websites choose to interact with ABC in ways that require ABC to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that ABC gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. In each case, ABC collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfil the purpose of the visitor's interaction with ABC. ABC does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

Aggregated Statistics

ABC may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites but does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. ABC uses cookies to help ABC identify and track visitors, their usage of ABC website, and their website access preferences. ABC visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using ABC's websites, with the drawback that certain features of ABC's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any acquirer of ABC may continue to use your personal information as set forth in this policy.

Ads

Ads appearing on any of our websites may be delivered to users by advertising partners, who may set cookies. These cookies allow the ad server to recognise your computer each time they send you an online advertisement to compile information about you or others who use your computer. This information allows ad networks to, among other things, deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by ABC and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.
